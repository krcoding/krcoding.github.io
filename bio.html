Bio

About the author of this site.

Hi, everyone! I'm Katherine Randazzo. You can call me Katie, Kate, Kat, Kath... Whatever you want, as long as it isn't Kathy. (No offense to the name, but it's just not me, you know?) I'm a student in the College of Wooster class of 2018. Currently I'm a junior, as you can guess from the fact that this is a site for my Junior IS, but that may change by the time you read this! I am a Comparative Literature with Computer Science Major (self-designed, not a double major). I have a French/English double minor.

Interests / Hobbies

Well, dear reader, I have a ton! My interests are all over the place. Some include...

  • Writing
  • Reading
  • Coding
  • Video Games
  • Nerd Culture
  • Animation
  • Foreign Languages (especially français and 日本語)
And many more!

Why did you make this website?

Another great question! This is actually a midterm project for a course I'm in this semester, Intro to Digital Humanities. So, that's pretty nifty. Plus, I kind of wanted to show off my IS idea to the world!

Anything else?

That's all I've got for now. Thank you so much for checking out my website, and I hope you enjoy learning more about my IS!
