Bio

About the author of this site.

Hi, everyone! I'm Katherine Randazzo. I figured that using a pretty galaxy image on my bio page would be stellar. (Source here.)

You can call me Katie, Kate, Kat, Kath... Whatever you want, as long as it isn't Kathy. (No offense to the name, but it's just not me, you know?)

I'm a student in the College of Wooster Class of 2018. Currently I'm a junior, as you can guess from the fact that this is a site for my Junior IS, but that may change by the time you read this! I am a Comparative Literature with Computer Science Major (self-designed, not a double major). I have a French/English double minor.
