A Junior IS by Katherine Randazzo.

An intensive research project examining narrative presentation in video games.

What is your IS about?

That's a great question, dear reader! Honestly, my IS is still a work in progress, so this website may end up being very different than the final product. We'll have to see!

Why Video Games?

Also a great question, dear reader! To be honest, it's because video games, in my humble opinion, don't get enough love as a genre. Especially Visual Novels. So, I figured I'd do my IS on them!

Will this also be your Senior IS?

Probably not. I'm planning on something really awesome, though--a visual novel based on literature, coded in the Ren'Py engine by yours truly. But, that's all tentative. We'll just have to see!

The Games I Chose

Since I'm a bit prone to motion-sickness, I went with games that do not have first-person camera perspective. Still, the large part of games I wanted to do didn't need that anyway! You can read more of the games that I've done on my Video Games page. Also, (Icon Credit in the footer links.)

My Research

I researched different kinds of genres, whether it's in connection to traditional literature, video games, or anime/manga. I also did an in-depth look into narrative, both what it means for humans psychologically, and what it means in terms of story presentation. (Icon Credit in the footer links.)

...Did you say anime and manga...?

You can see that screencap up there, right? Visual Novels, a genre that I adore, are very often influenced and based upon anime and manga, so it's only fair to examine those too! (Icon Credit in the footer links.)
